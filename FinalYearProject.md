## Purpose
- Align final year projects with AI-driven practices
- Shift focus from academic exercises to **products, patents, and startups**
- Ensure student employability through skill-based, industry-relevant projects

---

## Key Focus Areas
- **Mission-oriented projects** (3–6 areas) instead of scattered efforts
- **Product POCs → Patents/IP → Startups**
- **AI integration**: allow use of AI, but stress learning outcomes
- **Software engineering lifecycle** emphasized in execution
- **Cross-school collaboration** for multidisciplinary outcomes

---

## Faculty Development
- Training in AI implementation and evaluation rubrics
- FDPs on BASD, Python for Data Science, AI for Engineers
- Hands-on formats: Hackathons, Learnathons
- Continuous skill-building for project guides

---

## Project Pipeline & Industry Links
- **Capstone-to-Final Year continuity** for stronger outcomes
- **Cohort to Cohort continuity** TO ensure depth for projects, student to student mentoring
- **Industry problem statements**: SIH, IDEX, People+AI, Tata Innovent etc. Can be considered as Internships.
- **On-premises internships/consulting** with industry partners
- External collaborators for funded consulting & internships
- Policy framework for **IP sharing**
- Strictly avoid Copied / paid projects

---

## Product/Platform Strategy
- Develop **products for REVA use** (3 minimum per school)
- Suggested product areas:
  - Project execution management systems  
  - AI-enabled evaluation & grading  
  - Timetabling & optimization tools  
  - Hostel, mess, visitor, and peer management systems  
  - Research opportunities & repository tracking
- **Open-source platforms first**: Odoo, ERPNext, alternatives to SAP/MS365
- Strategic autonomy via open-source cloud & data sovereignty

---

## Evaluation & Tracking
- Updated **rubrics**: assess complexity, student understanding, viva performance
- Project/product tracking system to monitor progress
- Creative evaluation formats: YouTube demos, book publishing, etc.
- Success metrics:  
  - ≥10,000 users or ₹1 lakh revenue annually
